What happens when you reach out.

1

You send one email

A sentence is enough. "I think I need some support" counts. You'll hear back within one business day.

2

We talk for 15 minutes

A free, no-commitment call. You ask anything. If we're not the right fit, you'll leave with a good referral — promise.

3

You pick the pace

Weekly, every other week — whatever your life allows. Sessions are online, private, and yours.

How I work

Evidence-based. Grief-informed. Built for real life.

Sessions blend CBT, DBT, ACT, and motivational interviewing with strength-based, trauma- and grief-informed care. Practical skills you'll actually use, at a pace that respects where you are. Sessions are online, anywhere in New Jersey — most teens and young adults find it fits their lives better.

Questions

The practical things.

Do you take insurance?

Yes — through Headway. If I'm out-of-network for your plan, most PPO plans reimburse 60–70% of the session fee, and I provide the paperwork (superbills) to make that easy.

What are your fees?

Insurance via Headway; private sessions are $200. A limited number of reduced-rate slots are held for young adults.

Is everything online?

Yes — secure telehealth for clients located anywhere in New Jersey, with a private, confidential setup on your end.

My teen is the client. What do I get to know?

We set this up clearly at intake: session content stays private so therapy works; anything safety-related always comes to you. You'll never be in the dark about safety.
